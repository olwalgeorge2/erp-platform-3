# Customer Support Service

## 1. Purpose
1.1 Manage customer support cases, SLAs, and resolution workflows across channels.
1.2 Provide agents with full context, knowledge base access, and collaboration tools.
1.3 Track service quality, escalations, and customer satisfaction metrics.

## 2. Module Structure
2.1 `support-application/` - Handles case intake, routing, SLA monitoring, and workflow automation.
2.2 `support-domain/` - Models cases, interactions, entitlements, and escalation rules.
2.3 `support-infrastructure/` - Integrates with communication tools, knowledge bases, and ticketing channels.

## 3. Domain Highlights
3.1 Synchronizes customer profiles and entitlements from CRM and shared modules.
3.2 Supports omnichannel case capture with audit trails for compliance.
3.3 Provides feedback loops into product and operations contexts through eventing.

## 4. Integration
4.1 Triggers notifications via Communication Hub for updates and resolutions.
4.2 Shares case status with Operations Service and Commerce for coordinated responses.
4.3 Delivers service analytics to Business Intelligence and customer success dashboards.
